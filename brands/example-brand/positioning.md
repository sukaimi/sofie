# Kopi Kita — Brand Positioning

## Target Audience
- Urban millennials and Gen Z (22-38)
- Southeast Asia (Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia primary)
- Coffee enthusiasts who value local over global chains
- Active on Instagram, TikTok

## Competitive Position
- Premium specialty coffee, priced between mass market (Starbucks) and ultra-premium (Nespresso)
- Differentiated by local heritage storytelling
- Direct-to-consumer via social media and e-commerce

## Key Messages
1. "Real coffee, real stories" — authenticity over polish
2. "From our kampung to your cup" — heritage connection
3. "Specialty doesn't mean stuffy" — accessible premium

## Social Media Platforms
- Instagram: primary (feed posts, stories, reels)
- TikTok: secondary (behind-the-scenes, brewing tutorials)
- Facebook: tertiary (community engagement, events)

## Content Pillars
1. **Product** (40%) — new releases, brewing methods, bean origins
2. **Lifestyle** (30%) — coffee moments, pairing suggestions, café culture
3. **Heritage** (20%) — farmer stories, processing methods, regional coffee traditions
4. **Community** (10%) — customer features, events, collaborations
