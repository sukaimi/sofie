# Example Brand — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name
Kopi Kita

## Tagline
"Our Coffee, Our Story"

## Brand Summary
Kopi Kita is a modern Southeast Asian specialty coffee brand targeting urban millennials and Gen Z. The brand celebrates local coffee culture with a contemporary twist. Premium but approachable.

## Colour Palette
- **Primary:** #2C1810 (Rich Coffee Brown)
- **Secondary:** #D4A574 (Warm Latte)
- **Accent:** #E8B931 (Golden Crema)
- **Background:** #FDF6EC (Cream White)
- **Text:** #1A1A1A (Near Black)

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Playfair Display Bold
- **Body:** Inter Regular
- **Accent:** Inter Medium

## Logo Usage
- Minimum clear space: 1x logo height on all sides
- Always use on light backgrounds or with white knockout on dark
- Never stretch, rotate, or alter colours
- Preferred position: top-left or bottom-centre

## Tone of Voice
- Warm and conversational
- Proud of heritage, not preachy
- Uses casual English with occasional Malay/Indonesian words
- Never corporate or stiff

## Visual Style
- Warm, natural lighting
- Earth tones with golden highlights
- Lifestyle photography focus (people enjoying coffee, not just product shots)
- Clean layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle textures (paper grain, coffee stains as design elements)

## Do's
- Use warm colour grading on all photos
- Include at least one brand colour in every post
- Keep text minimal — let the image speak
- Show real people, diverse representation

## Don'ts
- No cold/blue colour grading
- No stock photo aesthetic (overly posed, white backgrounds)
- No more than 3 lines of text on any single image
- No competitor brand colours (#00704A Starbucks green, #DA291C Tim Hortons red)
- No alcohol imagery
